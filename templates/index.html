This project marries the centuries-old wisdom of the Thirukural with cutting-edge technology.
This innovative project utilizes natural language processing to make the ancient text accessible to a contemporary, global audience. By employing transliteration, it enables users to transcend language barriers, ensuring that the Thirukural's teachings resonate worldwide. This platform serves as a bridge between tradition and innovation, allowing users to explore the rich tapestry of moral and ethical lessons contained within the Thirukural's verses.
With its user-friendly interface and powerful search capabilities, KuralSeek revolutionizes the way we engage with classical texts, unlocking their insights for the digital age.
